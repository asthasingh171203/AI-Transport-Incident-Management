# Project Requirements

## Project Name

AI-Powered Transport Incident Management using ServiceNow

---

## Business Domain

Transport and Logistics

---

## Business Scenario

TransNova Logistics Pvt. Ltd. is a nationwide transport company responsible for managing vehicle fleets, cargo movement, warehouses, and driver operations.

The Operations Center receives multiple transport-related incidents every day. Manual incident classification and assignment increase resolution time and reduce operational efficiency.

This project proposes an AI-assisted Incident Management solution that helps service desk agents classify incidents, recommend priorities, suggest assignment groups, and provide knowledge-based recommendations.

---

## Scope

The project covers the following ServiceNow ITSM modules:

- Incident Management
- Knowledge Management
- Problem Management
- Change Management
- Reports
- Dashboards
- Flow Designer
- Business Rules

---

## Users

- Fleet Operations Team
- Warehouse Operations Team
- Driver Safety Team
- Maintenance Team
- Transport Operations Manager
- Service Desk Agent

---

## Expected Outcome

- Faster incident classification
- Improved assignment accuracy
- Reduced resolution time
- Better operational visibility
- Improved service quality
