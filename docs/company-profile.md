# Company Profile

## Company Name

TransNova Logistics Pvt. Ltd.

## Industry

Transport and Logistics

## Headquarters

Bengaluru, India

## Business Description

TransNova Logistics Pvt. Ltd. is a transport and logistics company that provides nationwide freight transportation and cargo delivery services. The company manages a fleet of vehicles, warehouses, and logistics hubs to ensure timely movement of goods across different regions.

To improve operational efficiency, the company uses ServiceNow ITSM for managing transport-related incidents such as vehicle breakdowns, cargo delays, route deviations, and warehouse operational issues. AI-assisted incident management helps the operations team classify incidents, prioritize requests, recommend assignment groups, and reduce resolution time.

## Objective

To build an AI-powered Incident Management solution using ServiceNow that improves transport operations by automating incident classification, priority recommendation, assignment, and resolution support.
